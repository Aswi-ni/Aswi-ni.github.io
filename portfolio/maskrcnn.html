Car Damage detection using Mask-RCNN

This project focuses on using artificial intelligence to automatically identify and highlight damage on vehicles through images. By applying a method called Mask R-CNN, the system doesn’t just locate the damage—it outlines the exact shape and area of dents, scratches, or cracks on the car. This kind of detailed recognition helps provide a more accurate picture of the vehicle's condition.

To make this work, the system first learns from a set of labeled car images where damage has already been marked by humans. Once trained, it can analyze new images and visually mark damaged spots on its own. This automation can be especially useful in the automotive and insurance industries, helping professionals quickly assess damage and make informed decisions with less manual effort.

By combining deep learning with real-world applications, the project demonstrates how AI can bring speed, consistency, and objectivity to a traditionally time-consuming process. It’s a practical step forward in smarter, faster vehicle assessments.
